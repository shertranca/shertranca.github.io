What is this?

Glad you asked! :) This is a private group (using the Slack platform) for people interested in learning more about how to trade cryptocurrencies. I've been a successful trader since 2014 sharing most things I do on Twitter and part of my story can be read by clicking here.

I started doing some 1 on 1 consulting sessions this year but I quickly realized that it could be somewhat expensive for most people and take too much of my time while not providing as much information as I wanted due to the duration of these sessions. Creating a trading group seemed only like the next obvious step. I won't stop using Twitter nor providing free information there of course, but I'll certainly spend a lot more time and effort here from now on.

I also realize I'm going to get some sort of hate for creating a trading group, mostly from people that think all information should come for free and also from some that wish they were doing it instead (envy), so let me illustrate why I don't agree with that:

◉ 1 - I spend a lot of time studying and researching cryptocurrencies to make better trades and to provide good information.
◉ 2 - Many have done it before me, for expensive prices even, and some of these groups have been quite praised.
◉ 3 - Hundreds of people have made money on top of information I provided publicly which can easily be confirmed by looking at tweets.
◉ 4 - In real life, many people pay for courses and information that can add value to them, so why do some people feel like they are entitled to receive everything for free when they are making money with it?

Let me make one thing very clear though: while we'll obviously discuss coins, entries, exits and everything, this is NOT A SIGNAL OR A PUMP AND DUMP GROUP! Differently from scammy groups like that (which can make you lose your money fast), we'll not be trying to SELL YOU coins, we'll not be charging you for an entry here just so you can buy our "bags". The focus on this group is to share materials, experience, knowledge, so you can decide for yourself on your trades and get better at them.

If you're looking for a get rich quick scheme, this group might not be for you. If you're looking to learn, as a beginner, intermediate or even advanced trader/investor, aiming for consistency and profitability in the long run, then I believe we can help. And when I say "we", it's because I'll have more people along the way helping me manage and provide content to this group.

Ah, it's worth mentioning we also like to keep it fun around here. We are not that regular boring economics class and we can be quite profitable! :D So, take a look at the "Join Now" section to find out what we cover exactly, and if you have any doubts don't hesitate to contact us.

To da moon!
